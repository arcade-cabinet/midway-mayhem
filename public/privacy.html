Privacy Policy

Midway Mayhem: Clown Car Chaos

Effective: April 23, 2026

The Short Version: Midway Mayhem is a local-only arcade game. We collect zero data. Nothing leaves your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking.

1. Data We Collect

Midway Mayhem does not collect any personal data. We do not track you, store your information on remote servers, or use analytics. Your gameplay is entirely yours.

2. Local-Only Storage

Your game progress, replays, and achievements are stored locally on your device only. These are stored in your device's local database and are never transmitted to any external server or service.

  • Save files remain on your device
  • Player profiles exist locally only
  • Replay data is stored locally
  • Achievement records are kept on-device

3. What We Transmit

Midway Mayhem transmits nothing. There are no network calls home, no telemetry, no crash reporting, and no performance monitoring. The game runs entirely offline.

4. Third-Party Services

Midway Mayhem does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. All game logic and rendering happens on your device.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Midway Mayhem is designed for all ages and is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Because the game collects and transmits zero data, there are no privacy risks for children. Parents should note:

  • No account creation required
  • No personal information collected
  • No network communication
  • No in-app advertising or tracking
  • Gameplay and progress remain completely private to the device

6. Device Permissions

On mobile platforms (iOS and Android), Midway Mayhem may request certain device permissions to enhance gameplay:

  • Haptics (vibration): Used only to provide tactile feedback during gameplay. No data is collected or transmitted.
  • Screen Orientation: Used to lock the screen in landscape mode during play. This preference is stored locally only.
  • Device Information: Accessed solely to optimize rendering and UI layout for your specific device. No data is sent anywhere.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete any game data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the application (iOS/Android)
  • Clearing browser storage (web version)
  • Manually clearing the app's local database through device settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Effective" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of Midway Mayhem following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, you can:

  • Open an issue on GitHub
  • Review the source code at github.com/arcade-cabinet/midway-mayhem

This privacy policy is straightforward because our game is straightforward: local, offline, and respects your privacy by design.
