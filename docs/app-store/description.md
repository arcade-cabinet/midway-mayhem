# Midway Mayhem: Clown Car Chaos

## Tagline (30 char limit)

## Short description (80 char limit)

Cockpit-perspective clown-car racer down a Hot Wheels mega-track in a circus big-top.

## Full description (≤ 4000 char — App Store)

You're the driver of a polka-dot clown car. The track is a Hot Wheels mega-loop inside a circus big-top. The audience is screaming. The hood ornament is spinning. You've got one job: steer.

**Midway Mayhem: Clown Car Chaos** is a cockpit-POV arcade runner. Your car drives itself forward. The track gets faster, weirder, and denser with obstacles the longer you survive. Swerve to dodge oil slicks and mirror mazes. Honk to scare critters off the road. Hit ramps for barrel rolls and back-flips. Every crash drains your sanity. Every trick pumps up the crowd.

**What you do:**
- 🏎️ Steer — drag anywhere on screen (mobile) or use arrow keys (desktop)
- 📢 Honk — tap the horn button to scare the crowd, barkers, and stray critters
- 🎪 Survive — every obstacle is telegraphed, but NIGHTMARE is merciless
- 🎲 Swipe for tricks — barrel rolls, back-flips, combo chains

**Four zones, escalating chaos:**
- **Midway Strip** — striped tents, barker crowd, starter pace
- **Balloon Alley** — pink sky, pickup rush, near-miss bonuses
- **Ring of Fire** — flaming hoops, raid gates, speed crown
- **Funhouse Frenzy** — mirror walls, duplicated props, trick multipliers

**Difficulty scales to your nerve:**
- **I'm Too Silly** — honks like practice
- **Kazoo It Yourself** — the house recipe
- **Honk Me Plenty** — louder, faster, pay attention
- **Ultra Honk** — the crowd will not forgive a drift
- **Nightmare Midway** — permadeath unlocked; regret mandatory

**Daily routes, global leaderboard, ghost replays.** Come back every day for a fresh deterministic track seed. See your previous best as a translucent ghost car. Unlock cosmetic livery tickets and horn sounds.

**Every run is a story.** Every crash is a wipeout or a meltdown, never a menu. Every honk scares someone. Every trick feeds the chain. Drive fast. Honk louder.
