# Lore — Midway Mayhem

## The world

You're inside the greatest clown car at the greatest midway in the world. The carnival never closes; the crowd never sleeps; the track never ends.

Every race threads down a scale-model Hot Wheels mega-track that spirals through the interior of a Cirque-scale big-top. Stage lights hang from iron rigging high overhead. Red velvet tiered seats line both sides of the track. Barkers shout over megaphones between bends. Peanut shells crunch on the shoulders. Somewhere in the distance, a calliope never stops playing.

You, behind the polka-dot hood, are the star attraction. Drive fast. Honk louder.

## The CLOWN car

A red-and-yellow polka-dot micromachine with purple A-pillars, a gold arched windshield, chrome gauges labeled **LAUGHS** and **FUN**, a squirting-flower hood ornament that spins constantly, fuzzy red-and-blue dice hanging from the rearview mirror, and a big red horn button on the steering wheel. The horn plays a Cmin7 chord with a different random detuning every time you press it.

You do not know how this car steers. Neither does the car. That's the point.

## The CROWD

The crowd is the real opponent. Lose their reaction and you lose the run — **SANITY** drops every time the crowd senses fear. Fill **HYPE** through speed and **CROWD REACTION** through tricks, and you keep going.

The announcer on the PA channels the crowd:
- "THE CROWD GOES WILD!" when you mega-boost
- "OOOH THAT'S GONNA HURT" on big crashes
- "WHERE IS THIS CLOWN HEADED?" on sustained no-crash streaks
- "CROWD LOST IT!" when your SANITY hits zero and the curtain falls

## The zones

The track weaves through four distinct sections of the big-top. Each zone changes the props, lighting, obstacle weighting, and ambient music.

### 1. The Midway Strip

Classic carnival rows. Striped arches overhead, game booths flanking the track, painted signs for cotton candy and funnel cakes. Warm amber lighting. Easiest zone. Starter music: carousel waltz.

### 2. Balloon Alley

Clusters of mirrored helium balloons tethered in bouquets on both sides, strings trailing to the ground. Pastel sky sweeping overhead. Obstacle set leans heavy on gates — precision steering required.

### 3. Ring of Fire

Hanging rings of glowing orange-and-yellow fire that rotate slowly across the track. Deep red lighting, smoke in the air. Hammer hazards swing across lanes. No retreat, no forgiveness.

### 4. Funhouse Frenzy

Strobing neon mirrors, cartoon-shaped silhouette doorways, carousel calliope music pitched just slightly wrong. Everything is slightly too bright. Highest obstacle density. The crowd LOVES it.

Loop. Repeat. Escalate.

## The HAZARDS

| Hazard | Flavor |
|---|---|
| **Barrier** | Construction-yellow sawhorses across a lane. Solid hit — heavy sanity damage. |
| **Cone Cluster** | A pile of orange cones scattered by the previous clown to crash here. Glancing blow. |
| **Gate** | Two blue pillars with a banner overhead — must thread between them. Force-through. |
| **Oil Slick** | Dark puddle on the track. Lateral velocity gets hijacked. |
| **Hammer** | A giant carnival-game mallet swinging across a lane on a mechanical arm. Timing dodge. |

## The PICKUPS

| Pickup | Flavor |
|---|---|
| **Boost Ring** | Gold-glowing torus suspended mid-track. Temporary speed boost + HYPE+. |
| **Ticket** | Blue carnival ticket stubs. Currency for CROWD REACTION + future cosmetic unlocks. |
| **Mega Boost** | Rare floating purple icosahedron. Big speed spike + fireworks + big CROWD score. |

## The CLOWNSHIP

You are not THE clown. You are behind the wheel for the clown — the star of the midway's big show. Every run is a performance. Every crash is a gag. Every boost is a bit. The curtain only comes down when the crowd stops laughing.

If you're reading this before your first run: the only real instruction is to **commit to the bit**. It's not ironic. It's not parody. It's a clown car tearing down a Hot Wheels mega-track inside a circus big-top and the crowd is going ABSOLUTELY NUTS.

Drive fast. Honk louder.
